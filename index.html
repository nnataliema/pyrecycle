welcome to pyrecycle

recycling datacenter heat for a sustainable future

about pyrecycle

Pyrecycle aims to transfer waste heat from datacenters to municipalities, cutting heating/cooling costs for both parties with no additional energy input. With 98% effective heat exchangers, we'll simultaneously cool datacenters and heat communities. Datacenters will not have to use inefficient evaporative cooling, saving on both water and cooling.

why should you care?

By 2030, datacenters are expected to use 8% of the world’s energy and billions of gallons of water per hour. In the process, they release crazy amounts of heat and water vapor–exacerbating the greenhouse gas effect. Only a few miles away, businesses and homes pay for the very same heat and water that datacenters expel by the terawatt. This issue is too pressing and the solution is too obvious to ignore.

why us?

Physical solutions are being explored by Equinix and local district heating operators in Europe, but many companies overlook the biggest bottleneck of reusing waste heat from datacenters: the fragmented process of outreach, feasibility calculation, and implementation--lacking scalability. Our platform aims to streamline this process, making key physical and thermal data easily accessible to each party. Think Tinder for datacenters and heat-hungry businesses.
